The Fungi Colony has invaded the village of Ever-Ta and has scattered the Food Supply!  Hop-Ta-Hop joins our cause to bring justice against the enemy.  Hop-Ta-Hop can use it's whip to stun foes (rendering them harmless) for a short period of time.  Hop-Ta-Hop can also use the whip to travel across ridges by grappling stakes.

Goal: Gather Food Crates
