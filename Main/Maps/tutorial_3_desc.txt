Tinker Ta of the Mechani-Ta Academy

The best of the best, Tinker Ta was the top of the cream in the academy.  This witty Ta-Ta can operate the most complex of mechanics within split seconds.  With a monkey wrench in one hand and a spiffy cap on the head, Tinker Ta has come to aid the Ta-Tas in great times of Mahatta.

Goal: Rescue Baby Ta-Tas
